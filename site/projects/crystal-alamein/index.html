The developer

For over 15 years, Darak Group has built more than buildings. Across high-end residential, commercial and hospitality developments, the group is known for two things that matter most here — delivering on time, and choosing only the most strategic locations across Egypt.

The project

Not just a project — a destination.

In the heart of New Alamein, directly on the North Coast Road, Crystal Alamein brings together luxury, stunning sea views and smart coastal living. A panorama of the sea, the lakes, the towers, Alamein Downtown and the iconic tower — minutes from every service and facility. Signature architecture by EGAD Designs, with fully-finished hotel apartments held to a world-class hotel standard.

Discover elegance

A new vision of luxury.

At Crystal Alamein, elegance begins with design but doesn’t end there. It lives in the soft curves of every detail — the architecture, the open balconies, the high-end finishes, and the seamless connection to nature. Every corner is crafted with intention, because elegance isn’t just how it looks, but how it makes you feel.

Hospitality

Hospitality, built into everyday living.

Crystal Alamein goes beyond the idea of a typical resort residence by integrating hospitality thinking into the daily experience — from how the project is managed, to the quality of shared spaces, to the smoothness of the resident journey.

A hotel-managed standard of experience.

With hotel-management logic embedded into the project vision — and Urban Zen (by Azur) behind the everyday — operations, presentation and shared environments are maintained to a higher standard that protects both lifestyle quality and long-term value.
